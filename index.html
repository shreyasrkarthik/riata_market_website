Welcome to Riata Market

Riata Market is your neighbourhood convenience store and desi grocery in the heart of Austin. We carry a curated selection of Indian staples, spices, snacks and daily essentials at fair prices. Whether you need to pick up ingredients for tonight’s dinner or crave your favourite namkeen, we’ve got you covered.

We’re located at 12221 Riata Trace Parkway Suite #180, Austin, TX 78727 and proudly serve the Riata community and surrounding neighbourhoods. Stop by, say hello and discover a world of flavours close to home.
